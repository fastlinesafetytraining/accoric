# Using the Employee Table


## Objective

To manage the Employee table on the Employee Dashboard.

## Steps

1. Navigate to the **Employee Dashboard**.
2. Filter the table by name by clicking on either first name or last name in the table's header.
3. To the right, clicking on **Certifications** will expand into a list of the employees' certifications.
you may scroll through the list of certifications an employee holds. Click on any certifcation to view an image of it.
4. Click **View** to see the employee's profile page. Click **Add Certification** to upload a certification and its
corresponding information to the employee's profile. Click **Delete** to permanantly delete an employee from your account.

![An old rock in the desert](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-employee-dashboard-options.webp "Shiprock, New Mexico by Beau Rogers")

5. Clicking on an employee's profile picture will change its active status. A green outline indicates active, while a grey outline indicates inactive.
