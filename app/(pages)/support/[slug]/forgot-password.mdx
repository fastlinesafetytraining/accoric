# Resetting Your Password

## Objective

To reset your password.

## Steps

*Note: Secondary users must sign in themselves to change their passwords.*

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page.

![Forgot Password on Login](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-forgot-password.webp)

2. Enter the email address registered with your Accoric Management account.
3. An email will be sent to this email address witha link to change your password. The link expires after you reset your password.
4. You may also change your password from the **Settings** page. Enter and confirm your new password. Then, enter your current password and click **Update.**
