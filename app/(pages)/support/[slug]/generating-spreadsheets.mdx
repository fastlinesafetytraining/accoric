# Generating Spreadsheets

## Objective

To create a variety of spreadsheets from information uploaded to you Accoric Management Account.

## Steps
1. Navigate to **Employee Dashboard.**
2. Click Spreadsheet at the top-right of the Employee Table.
3. Specify the category you would like to create the spreadsheet for, and any personal information you would like included.

![generate Spreadsheet](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-generate-spreadsheet.webp)

4. **Certification Name:** You may create a spreadsheet by a specific certification name, filtering all employees in the specified category to those that hold the certification you indicated in the text filed.
5. **All Certifications:** You may create a spreadsheet for all certifications that all employees hold in the specified category.
6. **No Certification:** You may create a spreadsheet showing just the employees' personal information.
7. After specifying the type of filtering, you may indicate the expiration status. Ticking **Previously Expired** and specifying how many days in the future you would like to view certifications in the **Expiring In** number field, will display already-expired certifications, and those that are expiring in the amount of days you specified.
8. Name the spreadsheet, then click **Create.**
