# Adding Certifications to an Employee

## Objective

To add a certification to an employee.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Employee Dashboard.**
2. Click **Add Certification** in the far-right column in your Employee table.

![Add Certification From Dashboard](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-employee-dashboard-options.webp)

3. Add pertinent employee information, such as a name for the certification and an issue and expiration date.
4. Upload a font and back picture of the certification. *Acceptable formats are in .jpg, .png, and .pdf.*
5. Click **Upload**
