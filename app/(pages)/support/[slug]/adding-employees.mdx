# Adding Employees


##  Objective

To add an employee to your Accoric Management account

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Employee Dashboard**
2. Click **Add Employee.**
3. Add pertinent employee information, such as name, address, phone number, email address,
 job title, and emergency contact information.

 ![accoric screenshot](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-add-employee-form.webp)

 4. Add a Profile picture. (*Preferred picture formats are .jpg or .png*). When added, employees by default are active; profile pictures will have a green outline, indicating they are active. Click [Here](https://wwww.accoric.com/support/active-or-inactive-employees)
 to learn more about active/inactive status of employees.
