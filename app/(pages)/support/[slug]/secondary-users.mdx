# Secondary Users

## Objective

To create secondary users and manage their permissions.

## Steps

### <ins>Creating Secondary Users</ins>
1. Navigate to **Settings.**
2. Enter a valid email address and the full name of the secondary user you would like to add.
3. Click **Create.**

---

### <ins>Setting Access Levels</ins>
1. Change a user's access level by clicking on the access level.

![Access Levels For Secondary Users](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-secondary-user-access-level.webp)

2. **Full Access:** Enables access to all features on Accoric Management.
3. **Restricted Access:** Disables access to adding and deleting employees, but can create and delete certifications on employees' profile.
