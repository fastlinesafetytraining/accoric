# Employee Active/Inactive Status

## Objective

To use the active/inactive functionality properly.

## Steps

### <ins>From the Employee Dashboard</ins>
1. Navigate to **Employee Dashboard**
2. A green or grey outline will surround each employee's profile picture.  
**Green** indicates active status.  
**Grey** indicates inactive status.  
Click on the profile picture to change the status.
![Change Active Status From Dashboard](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-employee-active-status.webp)

---

### <ins>From the Employee Profile</ins>
1. Navigate to **Employee Profile** Page.
2. Locate the **Status** box. Tick either the active or inactive radio button.
![Change active status from employee profile](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-update-employee-active-status.webp)
3. Click **Update** at the bottom of the screen.


---

### <ins>From Categories</ins>
1. Navigate to **Categories.**
2. Locate the category in which the employee(s) you would like to edit are located.
3. From here, you may tick multiple employees at once. Scroll to the bottom of the list and change their status. Learn more about categories [in this page](https://www.accoric.com/support/using-categories) to learn more about Categories.
![Change Active Status from Categories](/public/Images/support-page-screenshots/accoric-management-update-category-status.webp)
